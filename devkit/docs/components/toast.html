Components / Feedback / Toast

Toast

Auto-dismissing notification with icon, title, message, close button, and status variants.

CSS+JS

Status Variants

Success
Your changes have been saved.
Error
Failed to delete the item.
Warning
Your session will expire in 5 minutes.
Info
A new version is available.
<!-- Success toast -->
<div class="dk-toast dk-toast--success">
  <span class="dk-toast_icon">
    <svg ...>...</svg>
  </span>
  <div class="dk-toast_content">
    <div class="dk-toast_title">Success</div>
    <div class="dk-toast_message">Your changes have been saved.</div>
  </div>
  <button class="dk-toast_close">&times;</button>
</div>

<!-- Danger toast -->
<div class="dk-toast dk-toast--danger">...</div>

<!-- Warning toast -->
<div class="dk-toast dk-toast--warning">...</div>

<!-- Info toast -->
<div class="dk-toast dk-toast--info">...</div>

Container Positions

Saved
Document saved successfully.
Update
New version available.
<!-- Toast container (placed once in your page) -->
<div class="dk-toast-container dk-toast-container--top-right" data-dk-toast-container>
  <!-- Toasts are inserted here dynamically -->
</div>

<!-- Position variants -->
<div class="dk-toast-container dk-toast-container--top-center">...</div>
<div class="dk-toast-container dk-toast-container--bottom-right">...</div>
<div class="dk-toast-container dk-toast-container--bottom-center">...</div>

Dismissing

Dismissing...
This toast is fading out.
<!-- Add is-exiting to animate out -->
<div class="dk-toast dk-toast--info is-exiting">
  <div class="dk-toast_content">
    <div class="dk-toast_title">Dismissing...</div>
    <div class="dk-toast_message">This toast is fading out.</div>
  </div>
</div>
