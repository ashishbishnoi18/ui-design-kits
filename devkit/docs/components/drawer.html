Components / Feedback / Drawer

Drawer

Slide-in panel from screen edge with backdrop, header/body/footer, and directional variants.

CSS+JS

Right Drawer (Default)

Settings

Drawer content goes here. Supports scrollable content with a fixed header and footer.

<div class="dk-drawer-backdrop is-open"></div>
<div class="dk-drawer dk-drawer--right is-open" data-dk-drawer>
  <div class="dk-drawer_header">
    <h3 class="dk-drawer_title">Settings</h3>
    <button class="dk-drawer_close">&times;</button>
  </div>
  <div class="dk-drawer_body">
    <p>Drawer content goes here.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="dk-drawer_footer">
    <button class="dk-btn dk-btn--ghost">Cancel</button>
    <button class="dk-btn dk-btn--primary">Save</button>
  </div>
</div>
